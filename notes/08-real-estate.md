# Real Estate

* Largest financial commitment for most households
* Emotional attachment to ownership
* Down payments = 20%, Closing costs = 3%-5%
* On average ownership is better than bonds/bills and worse than equity in terms of returns.

## Benefits of Renting
* Liquidity
* Location Flexibility
* Diversification of investments
* No maintenance

## Benefits of Buying
* Leveraged investment whose gains accrue to the owner
* Tax benefits
* Long term investment
* Financial control over asset improvements
* Income from renting
